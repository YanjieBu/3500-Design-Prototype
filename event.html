Kong Pao Chicken

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 200 g (7 oz) chicken (diced, preferably thigh meat)
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 2-3 dried red chilies (adjust to taste)
  • 3 green onions (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
Optional garnishes
  • Sesame seeds
  • Fresh cilantro
